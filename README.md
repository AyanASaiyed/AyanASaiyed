### 🌊 WaterApply — [waterapply.app](https://waterapply.app)

**WaterApply** tailors your resume to any job posting in seconds. Upload your master resume once, then let AI rewrite it for each application with keyword alignment and ATS optimization. Free Chrome extension for one-click tailoring on Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, iCIMS, BambooHR, UKG Pro, and Rippling. Your documents stay private and secure on Supabase.

> 💡 Give it a try at **[waterapply.app](https://waterapply.app)** and let me know what you think!
